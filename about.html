About Us

Founded in 2014, ManyPeople is a audio engineering small business with over a decade of experience in crafting bespoke audio solutions. They have grown from a two-person startup to a dedicated team of sound designers, recording engineers, and project managers, all passionate about mixing beautiful music.

Our Mission

To empower small and medium-sized studios with high-quality, affordable music that drive engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of innovative and high-quality audio engineering solutions, recognized for our passion, creativity, and commitment to empowering musical artists and studios worldwide.
